Welcome to Rentify

Rentify was founded in 2011 and has over 175,000 landlords using its services. It provides a platform for property management to access financial metrics for potential tenants, including contact information and screening question answers. The app also allows users to validate financial information during or after the application process.Rentify is a property management app that helps landlords manage and market their properties. It has two modes: Owner/Property Manager and Tenant. In Owner mode, the owner has full control of the property and the app automatically adds monthly dues and sends alerts for pending dues.
